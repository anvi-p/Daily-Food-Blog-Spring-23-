Daily Food Blog Website
Created by Anvi Padiyar (UID: 118775210)

Welcome to my Daily Food Blog website, created for the CMSC388J Final Project assignment. This is a food blogging site that allows people to post and share the meals they have during their day. The purpose of the site is to promote healthy eating and diets, and to make sure people don't skip meals. The site can also serve as a resource for learning about new restaurants or sharing recipes. In every post, users can provide details for each meal depending on whether it's homemade or from a restaurant. For homemade meals, they can describe the recipe and its ingredients. For restaurant meals, users can discuss the restaurant environment, service, pricing, and location. Posts can also have a photo attached with them as well as a way for other users to comment on them. This provides a way for the community to show support as well as ask questions about recipes and restaurants they may be interested in.
